About Us

Duxwell Printing was founded in 1990 by Mr. William Seto. He saw the need of printing service in the local area - Financial District and Chinatown.

ABOUT MR. WILLIAM SETO (FOUNDER)

He is a community leader and an active of many associations and organizations in the local area. He has worked in the printing business for over 50 years.

ABOUT MR. DAVID TSE (MANAGER)

David is the person that will take care of all your needs. He will respond to your emails and handle all calls, and walk-in customers.
